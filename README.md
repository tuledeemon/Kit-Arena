# ⚔️ Kit Arena - Official Tracker

Welcome to the official public hub for **Kit Arena**! 


> **Note:** The core source code for Kit Arena is closed-source. This repository serves as the central community hub for patch notes, bug tracking, and feature requests.

## 🎮 The Roster
Jump into the arena with custom classes featuring complex mechanics, including:
* **Cordelius:** Drag your enemies into a secluded 1v1 Shadow Realm.
* **JudeLow:** Manipulate the battlefield by generating sudden sinkholes and boxing in opponents.
* **Executioner:** Mark a specific target in chat and hunt them down for an instant execution.
* **Gambler:** Roll the dice for a chance at the Ultimate Jackpot (or lose your max health trying).
* *And many more...*

## 🐛 Found a Bug or Have a Suggestion?
We rely on player feedback to keep the mechanics balanced and latency-free. If you found a glitch or have a cool idea for a new Kit:

1. Click on the **Issues** tab at the top of this page.
2. Click the green **New Issue** button.
3. Give it a clear title (e.g., *"Bug: Gravity Crush Orb goes through shields"*).
4. Explain exactly how to replicate the issue, or detail your suggestion.
5. Submit it! Our development team will review it and tag it appropriately.
